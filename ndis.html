Working with NDIS Funding

Navigating NDIS Capital Supports for construction can be complex. Deca helps prepare quotes and documentation aligned to NDIS requirements — making the process clear for participants and families.

We work with registered NDIS providers, support coordinators, OTs, and directly with participants.
